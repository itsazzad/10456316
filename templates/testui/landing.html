Let's assess your English!

FluentIQ assesses your English communication skills. It takes about 10 to 15 minutes and is split into 4 areas:

  • Speech Sounds

    Record yourself reading a short paragraph.

  • Fluency

    Record yourself speaking on a given topic.

  • Comprehension

    Listen to audio clips of people speaking and answer some multiple choice questions.

  • Listening Accuracy

    Listen to audio clips of people speaking and type what you hear.
